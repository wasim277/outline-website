Age Suitability

Outline is a news summarization app that provides ~60-word summaries of articles from trusted sources.

We do not create or publish original news content. Content is sourced from external publishers, and Outline only displays shortened summaries for informational purposes.

Our app is rated 16+ on the App Store. While most content is general news, articles may occasionally cover topics such as politics, health, or world events that may not be suitable for younger audiences.

Parents and guardians should supervise usage by children under 16.
