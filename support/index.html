FAQs

What is Outline?

Outline is a clean, personal news reader that provides ~60-word AI summaries and lets you ask questions about any article. It's a fully native iOS app.

How do I summarize an article?

Open any story and tap Summarize to get a concise overview.

How do I ask questions about a story?

Tap Ask GPT to chat and get quick explanations or extra context.

How do I personalize my feed?

Use the category chips (Science, Tech, Sports, Health, etc.) to follow topics you care about.

Notifications / Daily Digest

Enable notifications in iOS Settings → Notifications → Outline to receive daily digests.

Troubleshooting

  • Force-quit and relaunch the app.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Update Outline to the latest version in the App Store.
