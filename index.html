About the Founder

Outline was designed and developed entirely by me as the sole founder. From the initial concept to the final implementation, every aspect of the platform has been built from the ground up.

The backend infrastructure utilizes AWS Lambda functions for automated news fetching, OpenAI integration for intelligent summarization, and a sophisticated S3 storage system with deduplication and categorization.

The iOS application is built natively with SwiftUI, providing a seamless and intuitive user experience. The personalization engine is designed to adapt to individual reading patterns for a truly customized news experience.
